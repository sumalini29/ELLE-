Desserts

Fresh Lemonade

Ingredients: Lemon, sugar, water, salt, mint leaves.

Procedure: Squeeze lemon into a glass, add sugar, a pinch of salt, and cold water. Stir well and garnish with mint leaves. Serve chilled.

Banana Milkshake

Ingredients: Banana, milk, sugar, vanilla essence.

Procedure: Blend banana with cold milk, sugar, and a few drops of vanilla essence. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

Rose Milk

Ingredients: Chilled milk, rose syrup, sugar (optional).

Procedure: Mix rose syrup into cold milk and stir well. Add sugar if needed. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients: Watermelon, sugar, lemon juice, mint.

Procedure: Blend watermelon chunks with a spoon of sugar and lemon juice. Strain and serve cold with mint on top.

Mango Lassi

Ingredients: Mango pulp, curd, sugar, cardamom.

Procedure: Blend mango pulp with curd, sugar, and a pinch of cardamom. Serve chilled for a rich, creamy drink.

Cold Coffee

Ingredients: Instant coffee, milk, sugar, ice cubes.

Procedure: Blend coffee, cold milk, sugar, and ice until frothy. Pour into a tall glass and serve immediately.

Orange Punch

Ingredients: Orange juice, soda, honey, mint.

Procedure: Mix fresh orange juice with a spoon of honey. Add chilled soda and stir gently. Garnish with mint leaves.

Coconut Water Cooler

Ingredients: Tender coconut water, lemon juice, mint leaves.

Procedure: Mix fresh coconut water with a few drops of lemon juice. Add crushed mint and serve cold.

Chikoo Milkshake

Ingredients: Chikoo (sapota), milk, sugar.

Procedure: Blend peeled chikoo with chilled milk and sugar. Serve thick and cold in a tall glass.

Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients: Strawberries, yogurt, honey.

Procedure: Blend fresh strawberries with thick yogurt and a spoon of honey. Serve chilled with a strawberry slice on top.
