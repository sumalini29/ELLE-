Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: Khoya, all-purpose flour, sugar, water, cardamom, ghee.

Procedure: Mix khoya and flour into a soft dough. Shape into small balls and deep-fry in ghee on low flame. Soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and serve warm or chilled.

Rasgulla

Ingredients: Milk, lemon juice, sugar, water, cardamom.

Procedure: Boil milk and curdle it using lemon juice. Drain and knead the chenna until smooth. Shape into balls and cook in boiling sugar syrup until spongy and light.

Carrot Halwa

Ingredients: Grated carrots, milk, sugar, ghee, cashews.

Procedure: Cook grated carrots in milk until soft. Add sugar and stir until it thickens. Add ghee and roast cashews separately. Mix everything and serve warm.

Payasam

Ingredients: Vermicelli, milk, sugar, cardamom, ghee, cashews, raisins.

Procedure: Roast vermicelli in ghee. Boil with milk until soft. Add sugar and cardamom, mix well. Fry cashews and raisins, add on top before serving.

Fruit Custard

Ingredients: Mixed fruits, custard powder, milk, sugar.

Procedure: Dissolve custard powder in a little milk. Boil the remaining milk and add sugar. Mix in custard and stir until thick. Cool and add chopped fruits. Chill before serving.

Besan Ladoo

Ingredients: Gram flour, ghee, sugar, cardamom, nuts.

Procedure: Roast gram flour in ghee on low flame until aromatic. Cool slightly and mix with powdered sugar and cardamom. Shape into round ladoos and garnish with nuts.

Rice Kheer

Ingredients: Rice, milk, sugar, cardamom, almonds, ghee.

Procedure: Cook rice in milk on low flame until soft and thick. Add sugar and cardamom. Stir continuously. Garnish with chopped almonds roasted in ghee.

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, cream, sugar, vanilla.

Procedure: Melt chocolate and let it cool. Whip cream with sugar and vanilla until fluffy. Fold in the melted chocolate gently. Chill for a few hours before serving.

Banana Sheera

Ingredients: Rava (semolina), banana, sugar, ghee, cardamom.

Procedure: Roast rava in ghee. Add mashed banana and cook briefly. Pour in water and sugar, stir until smooth and thick. Add cardamom powder and serve warm.
