Curd Rice

Ingredients: Cooked rice, curd, mustard seeds, green chili, curry leaves, ginger, salt.

Procedure: Mix rice with curd and salt. Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, add chopped green chili, curry leaves, and grated ginger. Pour over curd rice, mix gently, and serve chilled or at room temperature.

Plain Paratha

Ingredients: Wheat flour, water, salt, ghee.

Procedure: Knead flour with water and salt into a soft dough. Roll into flat rounds and cook on a hot tawa with ghee on both sides until golden. Serve hot with pickle or curd.
