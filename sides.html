Desserts

Cabbage Stir Fry

Ingredients: Cabbage, mustard seeds, curry leaves, oil, salt.

Procedure: Heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Add chopped cabbage and salt. Cook on low flame until soft. Serve as a light side with rice.

Egg Bhurji

Ingredients: Eggs, onion, salt, oil, pepper.

Procedure: Saut√© chopped onions in oil. Crack eggs into the pan, add salt and pepper, and scramble gently. Cook until eggs are done and fluffy.

Boiled Vegetables

Ingredients: Carrot, beans, peas, salt, butter.

Procedure: Boil vegetables until soft. Drain, add a pinch of salt and a small cube of butter. Toss and serve warm as a side dish.

Simple Chicken Fry

Ingredients: Boneless chicken, salt, pepper, oil.

Procedure: Marinate chicken in salt and pepper. Shallow fry on medium heat until golden and cooked through. Serve hot.

Beetroot Poriyal

Ingredients: Beetroot, coconut, mustard seeds, oil, curry leaves.

Procedure: Grate beetroot and cook in a pan with little water. Add salt, then temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves. Top with grated coconut and serve.

Curd with Cucumber

Ingredients: Curd, cucumber, salt, coriander.

Procedure: Grate cucumber and mix with curd. Add salt and garnish with chopped coriander. Serve chilled with rice or paratha.

Potato Stir Fry

Ingredients: Potatoes, salt, oil, cumin seeds.

Procedure: Heat oil and add cumin seeds. Add chopped boiled potatoes and salt. Fry until lightly crispy. Serve warm as a side.

Simple Fish Fry

Ingredients: Fish fillets, salt, turmeric, lemon juice, oil.

Procedure: Marinate fish with salt, turmeric, and lemon. Pan-fry in oil on medium heat until golden. Serve hot with rice or salad.

Plain Omelette

Ingredients: Eggs, salt, pepper, oil.

Procedure: Beat eggs with salt and pepper. Pour into a heated pan with oil. Cook both sides until done. Cut into strips and serve as a light side.

Steamed Broccoli

Ingredients: Broccoli, salt, olive oil.

Procedure: Steam broccoli until just tender. Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of salt before serving. Great with rice or pasta.
