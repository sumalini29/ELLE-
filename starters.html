Paneer Tikka

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, salt.

Procedure: Mix yogurt with spices and lemon juice to make a marinade. Coat paneer cubes in the marinade and let them rest for 30 minutes. Skewer the pieces and grill or roast until golden brown and slightly charred.

Chicken 65

Ingredients: Boneless chicken, corn flour, chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, curry leaves, yogurt, oil.

Procedure: Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt, then coat with corn flour. Deep fry until crispy. In a separate pan, temper curry leaves and toss the fried chicken in it for extra flavor.

Fish Fingers

Ingredients: Fish fillets, breadcrumbs, egg, lemon juice, pepper, salt.

Procedure: Marinate fish with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Dip in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs, and shallow or deep fry until crispy and golden brown.

Gobi Manchurian

Ingredients: Cauliflower florets, corn flour, all-purpose flour, soy sauce, garlic, chili sauce, oil.

Procedure: Coat cauliflower in a spiced batter and deep fry until crispy. Toss in a pan with soy sauce, chili sauce, and garlic. Stir-fry for a few minutes and serve hot.

Chicken Lollipop

Ingredients: Chicken drumettes, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, corn flour, oil.

Procedure: Marinate chicken with spices and soy sauce, then coat with corn flour. Deep fry until crispy. Serve with spicy sauce or dip of your choice.

Prawn Fry

Ingredients: Prawns, chili powder, turmeric, salt, lemon juice, rice flour, oil.

Procedure: Marinate prawns in spices and lemon juice. Coat lightly with rice flour and shallow fry until crispy and cooked through. Serve hot.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients: Button mushrooms, cheese, garlic, bread crumbs, herbs.

Procedure: Remove mushroom stems and fill the caps with a mixture of cheese, garlic, and herbs. Top with bread crumbs and bake until the cheese melts and the tops are golden.

Egg Devil

Ingredients: Boiled eggs, mashed potatoes, onion, spices, bread crumbs, oil.

Procedure: Cut boiled eggs in halves. Wrap each half in spiced mashed potatoes, roll in bread crumbs, and deep fry until golden. Serve with ketchup or chutney.

Cheese Corn Balls

Ingredients: Boiled corn, grated cheese, mashed potatoes, chili flakes, salt, bread crumbs, oil.

Procedure: Mix corn, cheese, mashed potatoes, and spices to form a dough. Shape into balls, roll in bread crumbs, and deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot with sauce or dip.
