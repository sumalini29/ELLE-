Mini Veg Sandwich

Ingredients: Bread slices, cucumber, tomato, butter, salt, pepper.

Procedure: Spread butter on bread slices. Layer thinly sliced cucumber and tomato. Sprinkle salt and pepper. Cut into small triangles or fun shapes and serve fresh.

Cheesy Pasta

Ingredients: Boiled pasta, cheese, butter, milk, salt.

Procedure: Melt butter, add milk and grated cheese, stir until smooth. Add boiled pasta and toss gently until coated. Serve warm with a little grated cheese on top.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients: Banana, flour, milk, sugar, butter.

Procedure: Mash banana and mix with flour, milk, and sugar. Pour batter on a hot pan with butter. Cook both sides until golden brown. Serve with honey or syrup.

Mini Idlis with Ghee

Ingredients: Mini idlis, ghee, salt, curry leaves.

Procedure: Steam mini idlis. Toss them in warm ghee with a pinch of salt and curry leaves. Serve warm as a soft and tasty bite-sized snack.

Fruit Skewers

Ingredients: Apple, banana, grapes, watermelon, toothpicks or skewers.

Procedure: Cut fruits into small chunks. Thread them onto skewers in a colorful pattern. Chill and serve with honey or a yogurt dip.

Veggie Fingers

Ingredients: Boiled potato, carrot, bread crumbs, salt, oil.

Procedure: Mash potatoes and carrots, season with salt, and shape into fingers. Coat with breadcrumbs and shallow fry until crispy. Serve with tomato ketchup.

Mini Dosa Rolls

Ingredients: Dosa batter, cheese, butter.

Procedure: Make small dosas on a tawa, spread a little butter and grated cheese. Roll them like a wrap and cut into bite-sized pieces. Serve warm.

Sweet Corn Chat

Ingredients: Boiled sweet corn, butter, salt, pepper, lemon.

Procedure: Mix hot boiled corn with melted butter, salt, pepper, and a dash of lemon juice. Serve in paper cups for easy eating.

Chocolate Dipped Strawberries

Ingredients: Strawberries, melted chocolate.

Procedure: Dip each strawberry into melted chocolate and place on a tray. Refrigerate until the chocolate sets. Serve cold as a treat.

Mini Pizza Bites

Ingredients: Bread slices, cheese, tomato sauce, capsicum, corn.

Procedure: Spread tomato sauce on bread, add chopped veggies and cheese. Toast in oven or tawa until cheese melts. Cut into small squares and serve.
